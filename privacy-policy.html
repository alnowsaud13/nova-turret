Privacy Policy

Nova Turret — Idle Dot Shooter · Last updated: July 4, 2026

Summary

Nova Turret does not itself collect, store, or share any personal information. Your game progress stays on your device. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple or Google. The game may show optional rewarded video ads served by Google AdMob, which processes limited device data to deliver those ads.

Information We Collect

None directly. Nova Turret does not require an account, and does not ask for your name, email, contacts, location, or photos. We do not operate our own analytics or tracking tools.

Game Data Stored on Your Device

The game saves the following information locally on your device only, so your progress is there when you return:

  • Run progress (coins, wave, upgrades)
  • Permanent progress (Cores, Gems, Store perks, best wave)
  • Settings (such as sound on/off)

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app deletes it permanently.

Advertising

Nova Turret may offer optional rewarded video ads (for example, watching an ad to revive after a defeat). These ads are provided by Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, Google may process limited device information such as a device advertising identifier, IP address, and general device data. We configure ads to be non-personalized where supported. Watching ads is always optional — the game is fully playable without ever watching one.

You can learn how Google uses this data at policies.google.com/technologies/ads. You can also limit ad tracking in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising).

In-App Purchases

Nova Turret offers optional in-app purchases (Gem packs) processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play. All payment information is collected and handled by Apple or Google under their own privacy policies. We do not receive or store your payment details. Purchases of virtual items (Gems, Cores, and Store perks) are for use within the game only, have no real-world value, and are non-transferable.

Children's Privacy

Nova Turret does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game contains no chat features and no social features, and ads are configured as non-personalized where supported.

Third-Party Services

The third parties involved are Apple (App Store) and Google (Google Play and AdMob). Their privacy policies are available at apple.com/legal/privacy and policies.google.com/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If the game adds features that change how data is handled, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date revised before those features go live.
