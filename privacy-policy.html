Privacy Policy

Nova Turret — Idle Dot Shooter · Last updated: July 2, 2026

Summary

Nova Turret does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your game progress stays on your device. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never see your payment details.

Information We Collect

None. Nova Turret does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or any other personal data, and does not use analytics or tracking tools.

Game Data Stored on Your Device

The game saves the following information locally on your device only, so your progress is there when you return:

  • Run progress (coins, wave, upgrades)
  • Permanent progress (Cores, Gems, Store perks, best wave)
  • Settings (such as sound on/off)

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app deletes it permanently.

In-App Purchases

Nova Turret offers optional in-app purchases (Gem packs) processed by Apple's App Store or Google Play. All payment information is collected and handled by Apple or Google under their own privacy policies. We do not receive or store your payment details. Purchases of virtual items (Gems, Cores, and Store perks) are for use within the game only, have no real-world value, and are non-transferable.

Children's Privacy

Nova Turret does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. The game contains no chat features, no social features, and no third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

The only third parties involved are Apple (App Store) and Google (Google Play), which handle app distribution and payments. Their privacy policies are available at apple.com/legal/privacy and policies.google.com/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If the game ever adds features that change how data is handled, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date revised before those features go live.
